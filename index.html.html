Fantasy Finance LeagueInvestor-ready concept (one page)

“Fantasy Football for Investing — draft a lineup, compete weekly, build real habits.”
Vision (A+B): a fun gamified portfolio tracker that becomes a competitive head‑to‑head league product.

Problem

Investing is confusing, lonely, and not sticky. Portfolio trackers don’t build habits through competition.

Solution

Draft positions (QB/OL/WR/etc.), track weekly performance, and win matchups — while learning portfolio construction.

MVP Features

• Lineup builder (allocations + caps)
• Weekly matchups + standings
• Team pages (rosters + scores)
• Share links + league invites
• Education nudges (coach tips)

Business Model

• Subscription leagues ($5–$10/mo)
• Sponsored leagues (brands/creators)
• Premium analytics add-ons
• Future: brokerage integrations & revenue share

Why We Win

The moat is the metaphor + league format. People don’t just “track” — they compete weekly, share, and return.
